## Summary of significant errors found

1. IMO Prize laureate paragraph has at least 5 wrong years and Charney's "posthumous 1980" claim is doubly wrong (alive in 1971, IMO prize was 1971).
2. RICO-20 letter date: post says 17 September 2015; correct is 1 September 2015.
3. Charney 1969 paper: post body says it's a QJRMS paper on Hadley circulation; actually a JAS paper titled "A Further Note on Large-Scale Motions in the Tropics". Footnote citation is correct.
4. Shukla 1975 paper attributed to GFDL post-doc; actually MIT-era work published before Shukla's GFDL post-doc began.
5. Education dates (MSc 1965-67) inconsistent with Wikipedia/GMU (MSc 1964).
6. Beowulf-1 date: "summer 1994" inconsistent with beowulf.org "early 1994".
7. IGES dissolution: post says "2014-2015"; the decision was made in 2013, with completion in 2015.
