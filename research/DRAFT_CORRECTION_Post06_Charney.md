Issue: This phrase is NOT in Phillips memoir. Phillips uses "the most ambitious of these efforts" in reference to the UNAF (antiwar fund), not GARP. The verbatim phrase about GARP could not be located in any source consulted.

Recommended fix: Either (a) drop the quoted phrase and paraphrase ("He organized GARP — the Global Atmospheric Research Program — perhaps the most ambitious international weather research effort of the era."), or (b) find the source. Suggested rewrite: `He organized **GARP** — the Global Atmospheric Research Program — and led the National Academy of Sciences committee that planned the first global weather observing experiment.`
