- **ECMWF official photos:** Skipped. ECMWF Flickr (flickr.com/photos/ecmwf/) is CC BY-NC-ND 2.0; incompatible with the PD/CC-BY/CC-BY-SA policy established in earlier posts. If a specific CESM visualisation is needed, email copyright@ucar.edu for clearance.
