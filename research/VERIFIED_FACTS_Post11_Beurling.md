## Summary of issues for correction

1. **"Distinguished biochemist" for Karin Beurling** — overstates. Wikipedia: "worked in a biochemistry lab at Princeton University" / "distinguished Ph.D. student from Uppsala University." Recommend softening.
2. **"Later T52d/T52e variants (introduced from 1942)"** — T52d introduced 1942-43, T52e debuted late 1944. Recommend "T52c from mid-1942, with T52d/T52e following through 1944."
3. **"By late 1943, the Germans had changed their methods and the window closed"** — German change started mid-1942 (Stewen leak June 1942, T52c July 1942); window largely closed mid-1943. Recommend "By mid-1943" or "Through 1942-1943."
4. **"Room 115"** — Princeton lore not in mainstream verifiable sources (Wikipedia/Acta). Beckman 2002 is the likely source. Recommend leaving as is if Beckman supports it, or remove room number if not in Beckman.
5. **Spelling "Carl-August" vs "Karl-August"** — both spellings exist; post follows Wikipedia (Carl), Acta Math uses Karl. No correction needed but worth a footnote if rigorously consistent with Acta.
